NLP Projects

Sign Language Interpreter

Learnings

Heres what we have learned...

  • Deep Learning: Gain expertise in deep learning techniques, particularly in computer vision and natural language processing, to accurately recognize and translate sign language gestures.
  • Human-Computer Interaction: Understand the principles of HCI (Human-Computer Interaction) to design a user-friendly interface that meets the needs of individuals who use sign language.
  • Data Privacy and Security: Learn how to handle sensitive user data securely, implementing strong privacy and security measures to protect user information.
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Develop cultural awareness and sensitivity to accommodate variations in sign language used in different regions and cultures.
  • Feedback Integration: Implement feedback mechanisms to gather user input and continuously improve the translation system.
  • Accessibility: Explore ways to make technology accessible to individuals with varying levels of sign language proficiency and abilities.
  • Machine Learning Ethics: Understand the ethical implications of using machine learning in this context, such as consent and responsible AI use.
